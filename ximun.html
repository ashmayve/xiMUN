About xiMUN

Forging the Next Generation of Leaders

xiMUN is a premier Model United Nations conference that challenges delegates to engage with the world's most pressing issues through rigorous debate, collaborative problem-solving, and the art of diplomacy.

Delegates step into the shoes of world leaders — crafting resolutions, building coalitions, and navigating the complexities of international relations in an atmosphere of intellectual rigour and mutual respect.
